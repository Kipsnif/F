About us

What started as a weekly meet-up between 3 friends who loved painting, has turned into so much more than that. 6 years later we're an active painting club with our own clubhouse right in the heart of Amsterdam's Bos en Lommer.

The Netherlands is a country which has brought forth some of the most famous painters of all time like Rembrandt and Van Gogh, with Amsterdam playing both a role in some of their lifetimes and in the life their work has gotten to play since. Museum like the Rijksmuseum, the Van Gogh Museum and the Centraal Museum are world renowed and an amazing inspiration to us all.

Our club has different groups that meet independly from each other to tackle different issues, from oil painting to drawing to nude life figure drawing. Check them out down below.

If you're someone who's always loved painting and are looking for a relaxed enviroment with similarly paint-crazy people to share your passion with, come join us!

On this page you'll find information about the different groups that meet up weekly to paint together. Click the 'Join us' button if you're up for some painting!
